Biography

I am an academic administrator with extensive experience in leading technical institutions. I have served as President of the Institute Innovation Council (IIC), Chairman of IQAC, and CEO of NABL Accredited Labs.


My academic journey is marked by supervising 70+ theses at UG/PG levels and receiving the "Young Scientist Award" from the Madhya Pradesh Council of Science & Technology.
